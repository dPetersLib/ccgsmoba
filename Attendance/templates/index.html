WHAT TO DO
WHEN YOU COME FOR MASS

1

Wear your Nose Mask

Before entering the church premises, be sure to wear your face/nose mask.
NO FACE MASK, NO ENTRY!
2

Temperature Check

Surrender your self for temperature check at the gate.
This is necessary to make sure no sick member is allowed inside the church premises.
3

Wash and Sanitize your Hands

After checking your temperature, wash and sanitize your hands at the designated post at the inner entrance of the church.
4

Register your Name

The next step is to mark your name for the mass.
We keep all these records to ensure the population inside the church is not above required.
